My Top 10 Favorite Benefits of Corporate Credit:

BENEFIT #1: Separates Personal Finances From Business
Having your personal and business finances lumped together is not only a poor business practice, it can leave A LOT of financing on the table and prevents you from maximizing your credit to its fullest potential.

BENEFIT #2: Maintain & Improve Business Cash Flow
An interruption in cash flow can kill a business. With good corporate credit, you insure the funds are always available to pay for necessities and run smoothly (even during a recession).

BENEFIT #3: Strong Credit Makes It Easier for Future Loan Approvals
Once you've established a corporate credit profile, it’ll become even easier to get approved for additional financing. The better your corporate credit – the more creditworthy your business appears to lending institutions.

BENEFIT #4: You Have 100% Control Over the Spending
Unlike some loans where you have to spend the money on a specific purpose, with most corporate credit it’s left up to you to choose where the money goes and what to spend it on.

BENEFIT #5: The Least Risky Way to Fund Your Business
Traditional loans can cost you an arm and a leg in interest. And signing with a personal guarantee could mean you end up destroying your personal credit (and losing your personal assets). By having good corporate credit and limiting your personal guarantees, you’ll never have to worry if the business doesn’t work out.

BENEFIT #6: The Ability to Work with Higher Credit & Loan Limits
Once you’ve established good corporate credit, lending institutions will see that you’re creditworthy with the ability to pay on time and will begin to approve you for higher credit & loan limits. Within 6 months it’s not uncommon for companies to secure $50,000-$100,000+ in financing.

BENEFIT #7: It’s Easier Than Traditional Funding Qualifications
Traditional funding methods are backed by your SSN and rely more heavily on your personal credit, cash flow and collateral.

BENEFIT #8: Corporate Credit Offers Lower Interest Rates
Lenders like to see that you have consistent cash flow to pay bills on time and that you have no delinquencies on your record. Lenders are more likely to offer favorable loan terms to businesses that have a strong financial history.

BENEFIT #9: You’ll Get Better Terms on Credit Offers
From higher credit limits to lower interest rates and more favorable terms, corporate credit is just the smarter way to grow your business. If you’ve had accounts established for a while you may want to re-negotiate for better terms.

BENEFIT #10: It Leads to Better Business Building & Management
As an owner it’s your duty to do what is in the best interest of growing your business. By making credit a priority you’ll be growing your company the fastest and most efficient way possible.
