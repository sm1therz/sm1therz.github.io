Task

The tyres of the Lunar Roving Vehicle have also been brought into question, as many think they would be likely to explode in the vacuum of space. Again, a simple bit of homework reveals that the tyres were not of the traditional, inflated rubber kind. [8.5]

There, the problem was to identify the simple argument.  Here, we take it further.  Exercise 2.4 is to identify any hidden premise(s) and to produce a properly structured argument map showing all co-premises.
