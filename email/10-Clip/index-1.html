The font-size of <example-element> is set to clamp(16px, 2.5vw, 40px).

  • This means that the font-size will be set to 16px, until the computed value of 2.5vw becomes greater than 16px.
  • When the computed value of 2.5vw becomes greater than 16px, the font-size of <example-element> will be set to 2.5vw, until the computed value of 2.5vw becomes greater than 2.8rem.
  • At this point, the font-size of <example-element> will be set to 2.8rem.

clamp() allows you to set a minimum and maximum value.
